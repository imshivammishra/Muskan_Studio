About Muskan

Craft, Heritage, Vision

The Designer

Muskan's journey into fashion began among the handloom clusters of Rajasthan, where she spent childhood summers watching artisans weave stories into silk. This intimate connection with textile craft became the foundation of her design philosophy.

After graduating from Parsons School of Design in New York and completing a residency at Chambre Syndicale de la Couture Parisienne, Muskan established her eponymous atelier — bridging the rich artisanal traditions of India with the precision of Parisian haute couture.

Her work has been featured in Vogue Italia, Harper's Bazaar, and W Magazine. She was shortlisted for the LVMH Prize in 2024 and named one of Vogue India's Designers to Watch.

Philosophy

The Art of Quiet Luxury

✦

Heritage

Every collection honors centuries-old Indian textile techniques — from Banarasi weaving to Chikankari embroidery — reimagined through a contemporary luxury lens.

✦

Craft

Each piece is crafted by hand in our Paris and Mumbai ateliers. No detail is too small, no stitch without intention. True luxury lives in the invisible details.

✦

Sustainability

We work exclusively with ethically sourced materials and partner with artisan cooperatives, ensuring every garment supports the communities that inspire it.
